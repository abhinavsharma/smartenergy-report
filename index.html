About

Bosch Smart Energy is a research-stage product that helps users obtain home energy data broken down by appliance usage. Additionally, it allows viewing real-time energy usage data. For our BHCI Capstone Project, we developed a mobile website to help users visualize home energy usage and make more financially and energy conscious decisions about their energy usage. This webpage is a report of our most important findings
